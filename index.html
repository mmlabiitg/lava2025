About the School

LAVA 2025 is an intensive 8-day Summer School on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) for Computer Vision and Language Modelling. It features expert lectures, tutorials, and hands-on sessions led by top academicians and industry experts.

In this year’s edition, we are proud to introduce Industry Interaction Sessions, offering participants valuable insights into how AI/ML is applied in real-world scenarios by leading IT companies. These sessions will help participants prepare for careers in the dynamic and rapidly evolving AI/ML field.

Whether you're an aspiring student, early-career researcher, industry professional or faculty, join LAVA 2025 to elevate your skills and prepare for the dynamic future of AI/ML!

Our Speakers

Our distinguished speakers for LAVA 2025 will include leading researchers and industry experts from top academic institutions and renowned technology companies. They will share their insights on cutting-edge developments in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Accomodation

A limited number of hostel rooms are reserved for participants from 29th June to 8th July on a first-come-first-served basis. If you require accommodation, please select the appropriate option in the registration form. Further details will be communicated closer to the event.

Room Details: Shared 2/3-seater rooms at approximately ₹200 per day. Charges apply for the entire duration of stay, including arrival and departure days. An additional ₹50–₹100 per day are required for renting bedding and accessories. Participants are advised to bring their own bedsheets and mosquito nets.

Dining Options: Participants may dine at any open hostel mess or campus canteen. Messes provide breakfast, lunch, and dinner at ₹60–₹80 per meal (vegetarian). Non-vegetarian options incur additional charges. Canteens offer extended hours and a wider menu but are limited in number.
